Data Deletion Instructions

If you used scrapyUA services (including Threads integrations) and want your personal data deleted, follow one of the options below.

Option A — self-service via Threads/Instagram

  1. Open the Threads or Instagram app where you granted access.
  2. Go to Settings → Security → Apps and Websites.
  3. Find scrapyUA in Active apps and tap Remove. This revokes tokens and blocks further access.

Option B — deletion request

Email wewebrarium@gmail.com with subject Delete my data and include:

  • Your Threads/Instagram username.
  • The email you used with our services (if different).
  • Scope of the request (e.g., remove analytics, remove conversation history).

We delete related records (tokens, logs, analytics) within 30 days and confirm completion.

What we delete

  • Access tokens and refresh tokens issued to scrapyUA.
  • Post/reply metadata stored for automations.
  • Operational logs linked to your account.

We do not store payment data; billing is handled by third-party processors.

Questions? Contact wewebrarium@gmail.com.
