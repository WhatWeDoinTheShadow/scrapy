scrapyUA Privacy Policy

This policy explains how we handle personal data for our services and Threads integrations.

Data We Collect

  • Threads/Instagram account identifiers (user_id, username).
  • Access tokens issued via official OAuth.
  • Post/reply metadata required for automation workflows.
  • System logs for security and diagnostics.

How We Use It

Data is used only to deliver requested features: publishing, replying, and analytics. We do not sell data or share it with third parties except hosting/analytics subprocessors.

Retention

Tokens are stored encrypted with restricted access. Logs are kept up to 90 days unless law requires longer.

Your Rights

You can revoke access in Threads/Instagram at any time or request deletion at wewebrarium@gmail.com.
